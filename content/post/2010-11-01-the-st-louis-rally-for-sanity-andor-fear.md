We stopped by the Arch grounds on Saturday afternoon to see how <a href="http://punchingkitty.com/2010/10/08/st-louis-to-hold-satellite-rally-to-restore-sanity/" target="_blank">the St. Louis Rally for and/or Fear</a> was doing. The crowd for the &#8220;satellite&#8221; rally to the one happening in tandem at the Washington DC mall was larger than expected and sitting on the Arch steps watching John Stewart on a smallish screen setup, cheering at every joke and musical performance.

<p style="text-align: center;">
  <a href="http://media.punchingkitty.com/wordpress/2010/10/stl_rally_DSCF2956.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7665" title="stl_rally_DSCF2956" src="http://media.punchingkitty.com/wordpress/2010/10/stl_rally_DSCF2956.jpg?filter=resize&w=600&q=50" alt="" width="600" height="450" /></a>
</p>

<p style="text-align: left;">
  Though some missed the point and brought campaign posters to be intermixed with the awesome signs, the rally was a peaceful, entertaining time for everyone, no matter who you are planning on voting for Tuesday. Sure, there were a few nutjobs&#8230;
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
  <a href="http://media.punchingkitty.com/wordpress/2010/10/stl_rally_DSCF3001.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7661" title="stl_rally_DSCF3001" src="http://media.punchingkitty.com/wordpress/2010/10/stl_rally_DSCF3001.jpg?filter=resize&w=600&q=50" alt="" width="600" height="450" /></a>
</p>

<p style="text-align: left;">
  &#8230;but there were far more nice people, filling the Arch ground steps, having a good time&#8230;
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
  <a href="http://media.punchingkitty.com/wordpress/2010/10/stl_rally_DSCF2958.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7664" title="stl_rally_DSCF2958" src="http://media.punchingkitty.com/wordpress/2010/10/stl_rally_DSCF2958.jpg?filter=resize&w=600&q=50" alt="" width="600" height="450" /></a>
</p>

<p style="text-align: left;">
  The one problem for the local rally&#8217;s organizers was how do you clear the crowd after the DC rally telecast was over? We know! Have a few comics up on stage to make awkward jokes and talk about edgy stuff like how men and women are different!
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
  <a href="http://media.punchingkitty.com/wordpress/2010/10/stl_rally_DSCF3014.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7655" title="stl_rally_DSCF3014" src="http://media.punchingkitty.com/wordpress/2010/10/stl_rally_DSCF3014.jpg?filter=resize&w=600&q=50" alt="" width="600" height="450" /></a>
</p>

<p style="text-align: left;">
  There you go! That did it. Plenty of room now to ride your weird bike&#8230;
</p>
