As promised, we stopped by the Con-tamination Horror Movie, Sci-Fi, Pop Culture convention this weekend to see the happenings of St. Louis&#8217; horror-loving scene.

In summary, the coolest thing was easily the Night of the Living Dead puppet show put on by <a href="http://www.angryyoungmenltd.com/" target="_blank">Angry Young Men, Ltd</a>

The scariest thing we saw? <a href="http://punchingkitty.com/?attachment_id=4185" target="_blank">This autographed photo of David Caruso</a>. Why would someone want that?! Creepy to think about.

After the jump, we have the Â best 100-ish photos from the events, but for those that care a little less and want the overview, the photo montage video directly below is the way to go.
